All About Dolphins

The Wonderful Mammals of the Ocean

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their sociability and communication skills.
They belong to the cetacean family and are fascinating to both scientists and the public.

Species of Dolphins

  • Common Dolphin
    • Habitat: Temperate and tropical waters
    • Characteristics: Gray color and fast swimmer
  • Bottlenose Dolphin
    • Habitat: Oceans worldwide
    • Characteristics: Famous for playful behavior
  • Risso's Dolphin
    • Habitat: Deep waters
    • Characteristics: Robust body and wrinkled skin

Fun Facts

  1. Dolphins can live up to 40 years.
    1. Life expectancy varies by species.
  2. They communicate using a variety of sounds.
  3. They can swim at speeds of up to 60 km/h.
